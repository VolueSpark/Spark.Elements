## Usage

The forecast components are used to show prognosis with average prices for the next seven days, which allows users to compare prices for each timeslot against today’s average prices. The timeslots are adjustable, and gives EV owners the information they need in order to decide when’s the best day or timeslots to charge their vehicle.
